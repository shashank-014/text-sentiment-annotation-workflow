# Sentiment Annotation Guidelines

## Objective
Label each review based on overall sentiment.

## Labels

- Positive → Clearly expresses satisfaction or approval.
- Negative → Clearly expresses dissatisfaction or criticism.
- Neutral → Factual, mixed, or emotionally balanced statement.

## Annotation Rules

1. Label based on overall tone.
2. If both positive and negative appear, select dominant tone.
3. Do not assume meaning beyond written text.
4. Maintain consistency across similar phrases.
5. Avoid personal bias.

## Examples

| Review | Label |
|--------|--------|
| Loved the service | Positive |
| Very bad support | Negative |
| Product arrived yesterday | Neutral |
